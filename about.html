Making Donuts

Donuts aren't really hard to make. They're basically sweet bread with sugar(or chocolate) on top with usually a filling of nutella, jam, bueno, white chocolate e.g. to make the glace over them you could use sugar, egg whites and little vanilla, then freeze them (but not for too long) and then put them on your donut. After you put it you could freeze the donut with the glace on it (again) and there you have it.
